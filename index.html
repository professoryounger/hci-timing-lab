HCI Timing Lab

A trio of quick experiments for Chapter 13: The Timely User Experience.

  1. Reaction Time: Click when the panel turns green.
  2. Perceived Delay: Estimate response time after a hidden/known lag.
  3. Progress Illusion: Same 1 s duration, different pacing. Which feels fastest?

Results are stored locally in your browser until cleared. Use the navbar buttons to download CSV for analysis.
