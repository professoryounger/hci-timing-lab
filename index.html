HCI Timing Lab

A trio of quick experiments for Chapter 13: The Timely User Experience.

  1. Reaction Time: Click when the screen turns green.
  2. Perceived Delay: Estimate system response after known lags.
  3. Progress Illusion: Same duration, different pacing. Which feels faster?
